Telling story through fragrance

Founded in 2010, SaffCoZ started in a small atelier in Indonesia, where we handcrafted our first collection using ethically sourced ingredients, inspired by the wealth and cultures of the world. We believe that fragrance is much more than just a scent—it's a story, a memory, and a journey captured in a bottle.


Each of our fragrances are meticulously crafted by master perfumers, ensuring each fragrance has their own characters. From delicate, floral notes to sweet and fruity, to bold and musky, we aim to create a lasting impressions for every wearer. May every wearer has their own scent that tell their stories to the world.


At SaffCoZ, we are committed to using eco-friendly practices and cruelty-free ingredients, ensuring our products honor both nature and environments. SaffCoZ is more than a brand—it's a promise to always deliver timeless scents that resonate with the heart and soul. Let us take you on a sensory journey where every drop tells a story.

Why Us?

Each fragrance created by perfumers in SaffCoZ prioritized sustainability to honor the nature that comes before us. Each fragrance are created with care, love, and passion, so it resonates with your soul and heart.
